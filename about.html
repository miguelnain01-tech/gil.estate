About Miguel

A Personal Approach to Exceptional Real Estate

Real estate is more than transactions—it's about understanding dreams, recognizing potential, and guiding people to places where their next chapter unfolds. This belief has shaped every aspect of my approach to luxury real estate in San Diego.

My journey in real estate began with a simple observation: the most successful transactions happen when there's genuine trust between agent and client. In the luxury market, where discretion and expertise are paramount, this trust becomes even more essential.

I've dedicated myself to becoming an authority on San Diego's most prestigious communities—from the coastal elegance of La Jolla to the private estates of Rancho Santa Fe. Each neighborhood has its own character, its own rhythm, and its own unique appeal. Understanding these nuances allows me to match clients not just with properties, but with lifestyles.

What sets my approach apart is the combination of deep local knowledge with a commitment to personalized service. I believe that every client deserves my full attention, every question deserves a thoughtful answer, and every transaction deserves meticulous care.

When I'm not helping clients find their perfect home, you'll find me exploring San Diego's hidden gems—whether that's a new restaurant in the Gaslamp Quarter, a hiking trail in Torrey Pines, or simply watching the sunset from Sunset Cliffs. This love for the region informs everything I do and helps me share the authentic San Diego experience with those looking to make it their home.
