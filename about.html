About Miguel

A Personal Approach to Exceptional Real Estate

Real estate is more than transactions—it's about understanding dreams and guiding people to places where their next chapter unfolds. Born and raised in San Diego, I know this city from the inside out. My Navy service instilled integrity and discipline—values I bring to every client. I'm proud to work with veterans and active-duty members, specializing in VA loans to help those who served.

My journey in real estate is built on trust. I've dedicated myself to becoming an authority on San Diego's most prestigious communities—from La Jolla to Rancho Santa Fe—matching clients not just with properties, but with lifestyles. Every client deserves full attention, every question deserves a thoughtful answer, and every transaction deserves meticulous care.

When I'm not helping clients, you'll find me exploring San Diego's hidden gems—a new restaurant in the Gaslamp Quarter, a trail in Torrey Pines, or sunset at Sunset Cliffs. This love for the region helps me share the authentic San Diego experience with those looking to make it their home.
